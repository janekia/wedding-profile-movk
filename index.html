About me

Hi, I’m Janekia — the creator behind Pinkkard Design. I specialize in building websites that are not only visually appealing but also functional and easy to use. Whether you're a small business owner, a creative, or someone with a big idea, I’m here to help bring your vision to life online.

I started designing websites because I love blending creativity with problem-solving. I’ve built personal projects like my own resume site, ecommerce mockups, and a voice acting portfolio for my fiancé. Every project is a chance to learn, improve, and create something meaningful.

My goal is to make the web more beautiful and more accessible—one custom site at a time. When you work with me, you’re not just hiring a developer. You’re partnering with someone who truly cares about your project.
